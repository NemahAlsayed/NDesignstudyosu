What Is WhatConverts?

WhatConverts is an all‑in‑one lead tracking, call tracking, and marketing attribution software that helps businesses understand:

  • Which campaigns generate leads

  • Which channels drive phone calls

  • Which keywords convert

  • Which leads turn into sales

  • Which campaigns waste budget

It’s designed to give marketers complete visibility into the customer journey—from the first click to the final sale.

How WhatConverts Works

1. Tracking Every Lead Source

WhatConverts captures leads from:

  • Phone calls

  • Web forms

  • Live chat

  • eCommerce transactions

  • Events and conversions

It automatically identifies the marketing source, campaign, keyword, and landing page that generated each lead.

2. Dynamic Number Insertion (DNI)

For call tracking, WhatConverts uses Dynamic Number Insertion, which swaps phone numbers on your website based on the visitor’s traffic source. This allows you to see exactly which ad, keyword, or campaign triggered the call.

3. Lead Management Dashboard

Every lead—call, form, chat, or sale—appears in a centralized dashboard where you can:

  • View call recordings

  • Score leads

  • Add notes

  • Track sales value

  • Build custom reports

This makes it easy for agencies and businesses to measure ROI and optimize campaigns.

4. Marketing Attribution & Customer Journey

Higher‑tier plans include multi‑click attribution and customer journey tracking, showing:

  • All touchpoints before conversion

  • Pages visited

  • Channels involved

  • Full lead intelligence

This is especially valuable for businesses with long sales cycles.
